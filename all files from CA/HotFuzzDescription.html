Hot Fuzz

Action

Simon Pegg, Nick Frost, Timmy Dalton, Paddy Considine, Jim Broadbent, Bill Nighy, Olivia Colman, Martin Freeman



Hot-shot police officer Nicholas Angel smells foul play when the residents of a sleepy Somerset village start to die in seemingly terrible accidents.
Something is afoot, and he must work with daft local plod Danny Butterman to find out what.
