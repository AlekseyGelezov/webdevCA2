Alien

Thriller

Sigourney Weaver, Tom Skerrit, Veronica Cartwright, John Hurt, Harry Dean Stanton, Ian Holm, Bolaji Badejo, Yaphet Kotto



In deep space, the crew of the commercial starship Nostromo is awakened from their cryo-sleep capsules halfway through their journey home to investigate a distress call from an alien vessel.

The terror begins when the crew encounters a nest of eggs inside the alien ship.
